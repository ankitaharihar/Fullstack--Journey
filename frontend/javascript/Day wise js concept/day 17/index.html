Promises

Promises are objects that represent the eventual completion (or failure) of an asynchronous operation and its resulting value. 1] Pending: The initial state of a promise. The operation has not completed yet. 2] Fulfilled: The operation completed successfully, and the promise has a resulting value. 3] Rejected: The operation failed, and the promise has a reason for the failure (usually an error).
